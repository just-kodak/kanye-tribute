Creative Projects

The section discusses about some of Kanye West Creative Projects outside his music and clothing brand

Donda Academy

INFO: Donda Academy was a private Christian school founded by Kanye West in 2022, located in simi valley, California. Named after hid late mother, Donda West, the institution aimed to"prepare students to become the next generation of leaders, thinkers, and innovators"

Stem Player

INFO: The Stem Player is a portable audio device developed by Kanye West in collaboration with british tech company Kano Computing.

Sunday Service

INFO: Sunday Service is a musical and spiritual gathering initiated by Kanye West in january 2019. These events feature gospel renditions of Kanye's songs and other spiritual music, performed by the Sunday Service Choir under the direction of choir director Jason White
